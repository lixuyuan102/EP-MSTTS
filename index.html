EP-MSTTS

Xuyuan Li*1, Zengqiang Shang *1
1 UCAS
2 HCCL IOA

Abstract

Neural end-to-end Text-to-Speech(TTS) systems have been able to generate high-quality single-sentence speech with highly expressive. However, reconstructing highly expressive, long-duration speech for paragraph-level text remains a challenge. The paragraph speech synthesized by the existing model cannot express the style of each sentence well. In addition, training models directly on these overly long speeches can affect the quality of the generated speech. In this paper, we propose a highly expressive paragraph speech synthesis system with multi-step variational autoencoder(EP-MSTTS). This system decouples speech information at different grammatical levels into latent variables and predicts them separately to synthesize high-quality paragraph speech. We also propose a three-step training method to help the whole decoupling process. The proposed TTS model was trained on a 30-hour dataset of French novel recordings. Experimental result shows that our model can produce higher sound quality and more expressive paragraph speech compared to VITS and conditional VITS with the BERT embedding of paragraph text.
